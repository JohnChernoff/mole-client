Molechess stores and uses only information you give for the purpose of playing Molechess.
This includes your Lichess account name and blitz rating as well as any Molechess preferencess you choose to create.

Molechess does not store or use any passwords associated with Lichess (or any other site).

This privacy policy applies to both the Molechess Web App (molechess.com) and Molechess Mobile on the Google Play Store.
